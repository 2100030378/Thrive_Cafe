Espresso

A concentrated coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans. It has a rich, intense flavor and a layer of golden crema on top.

Latte

Made with a shot of espresso and steamed milk, topped with a small amount of milk foam. It has a creamy texture with a balanced coffee flavor.

Cappuccino

Prepared with equal parts of espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. It has a strong coffee flavor with a luxurious, frothy texture.

Americano

Made by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water. It has a milder flavor than espresso but retains its boldness.

Mocha

A combination of espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup, often topped with whipped cream. It offers a perfect balance of coffee and chocolate flavors.

Macchiato

Espresso "stained" with a small amount of steamed milk. It has a strong coffee flavor with a hint of creaminess from the milk.

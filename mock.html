Explore the variety of mocktails waiting for you Thrive



Virgin Mojito


Muddle lime and mint, add simple syrup, ice, and top with club soda.

Watermelon Agua Fresca

Blend watermelon, lime juice, and honey, strain, mix with water, serve over ice.

Strawberry Basil Lemonade

Blend strawberries, basil, lemon juice, and honey, strain, mix with water, serve over ice.

Pineapple Coconut Cooler

Shake pineapple juice, coconut milk, lime juice, and syrup, strain over ice, top with club soda.

Cucumber Mint Cooler

Muddle cucumber and mint with lime juice and syrup, add ice, top with club soda, garnish.

Berry Sparkler

Muddle mixed berries with lemon juice and syrup, add ice, top with sparkling water, garnish.
